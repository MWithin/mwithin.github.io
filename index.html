The Geometry of Berkovich Analytic Spaces
Master's Thesis, 2022
Berkovich spaces are a type of analytic space over non-Archimedean fields. This project looked at various techniques to determine aspects of their structure - in particular, the notion of a skeleton.

Studied the classification theorem for compact analytic manifolds over the p-adics, and completed Serre's proof-sketch into a full proof, using only direct methods from topology and non-Archimedean analysis.
